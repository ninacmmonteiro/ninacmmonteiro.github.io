About Me

As a certified full-spectrum doula, I believe in the power of informed choice and compassionate care throughout your pregnancy journey. My approach centers on creating a safe, supportive environment where you feel empowered to make decisions that align with your values and birth preferences.


I provide evidence-based information, emotional support, and advocacy to help you navigate this transformative time with confidence. Whether you're planning a hospital birth, home birth, or anything in between, I'm here to support your unique path to parenthood.

Services Offered

Birth Support

Continuous emotional, physical, and informational support during labor and delivery. I help create a calm, supportive environment while advocating for your birth preferences.

Prenatal Education

Comprehensive childbirth education, birth planning sessions, and resources to help you prepare for your birthing experience with confidence and knowledge.

Postpartum Care

Support during the fourth trimester including newborn care guidance, emotional support, and resources for your recovery and adjustment to parenthood.

Lactation Support

Guidance and support for breastfeeding success, including proper latch techniques, positioning, and troubleshooting common challenges.
